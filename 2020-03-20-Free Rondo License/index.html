Free Rondo Licence

20 Mar 2020

Since Rondo won’t work on the latest MacOS ‘Catalina’ and I haven’t sold licenses for a number of years now, I have made a free license available to anybody who would like one.

Please note that the software is completely unsupported and will not work on any MacOS later than 10.14.
